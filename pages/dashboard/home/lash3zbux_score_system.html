  • Payout ‚Üí X: X = payout √∑ bet (e.g., $36.80 on $0.40 bet = 92.00x).

L3Z Battleground ‚Äî Scoring

Two games go head-to-head. You (or the market) predict which game posts the higher X. Admin sets the result once both are played.

  • Win: Higher X wins the battle.
  • Tie: If exactly equal, admin may mark a tie or use a tie-breaker (e.g., higher payout).
  • Markets: H2H winner, side props (e.g., any 300x+, total 100x+).
Your LBX payout is determined by the odds set for the winner market.

PVP ‚Äî Scoring & Bracket Flow

PVP is an East vs West bracket (8/16/32). Players enter with a game pick. Rounds advance toward the middle and a champion is crowned.

  • Round Win: Higher X (or admin-defined metric) advances to the next round.
  • Bracket: R1 ‚Üí Semi ‚Üí Final ‚Üí Champion (displayed big at the bottom).
  • Predictions: Exact Ending Balance, Highest X (value only), Lowest X (value only), Highest paying game (populated post-hunt).

Tie-Breakers & Fair Play

  • H2H ties: Admin may apply a tie rule (e.g., higher dollar payout, earlier achieved X, or replay).
  • Leaderboard ties: Broken by most recent points earned, then total LBX held.
  • Fair play: Account sharing, exploits, or spam entries can result in disqualification or LBX removal.

Rules may be adjusted slightly for special events; watch the stream or Discord for announcements.
